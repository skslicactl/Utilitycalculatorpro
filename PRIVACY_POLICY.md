# Privacy Policy - UtilityCalculator Pro

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Overview

UtilityCalculator Pro ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

---

## Information We Collect

### **We DO NOT Collect:**
- ❌ Personal identification information
- ❌ Contact details (email, phone, address)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Usage analytics
- ❌ Calculation history or data
- ❌ Financial information
- ❌ Browsing habits

### **We DO Access:**
- ✅ **Currency Exchange Rates**: Only when using the Currency Converter feature
  - Source: Public financial APIs
  - Purpose: Provide real-time exchange rates
  - Storage: Temporary cache only, cleared regularly
- ✅ **Local Device Storage**: For calculation history and app preferences
  - Stored locally on your device only
  - Never transmitted or shared
  - You control deletion through app settings

---

## How We Use Information

### **Local Data Usage:**
- **Calculation History**: Stored locally to provide you with previous calculations
- **App Preferences**: Settings like rounding modes and default units
- **Currency Cache**: Temporary storage of exchange rates for offline use

### **Network Usage:**
- **Currency Data Only**: Optional feature to fetch current exchange rates
- **No Tracking**: We don't track your usage patterns
- **No Analytics**: No data collection for analysis or marketing

---

## Data Storage & Security

### **Local Storage:**
- All calculation data is stored locally on your device
- Data remains private and under your control
- No cloud synchronization or backup
- Data is deleted when you uninstall the app

### **Network Security:**
- Currency API requests use HTTPS encryption
- No personal data transmitted
- Minimal data transfer (exchange rates only)
- Secure connection protocols

### **Data Retention:**
- **Calculation History**: Until you clear it or uninstall the app
- **Currency Cache**: Maximum 24 hours, then auto-deleted
- **App Settings**: Until you reset or uninstall the app
